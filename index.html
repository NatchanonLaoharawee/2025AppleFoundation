Key Ingredients:

Garlic Pork Recipe

This is my go-to recipe for an easy, delicious meal. Here's how you can make it:

  • Boil the pork with salt until tender.
  • Take it out, let it cool, and cut into pieces.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a pan and stir-fry the pork until golden brown. Enjoy!
